About Fabric Lakehouse

This environment follows Microsoft's Medallion Architecture in Microsoft Fabric. Data progresses through Bronze (raw source data), Silver (validated and transformed business-ready datasets), and Gold (curated views optimized for reporting, analytics, and the data that powers Argus).

Each layer improves data quality, governance, and reusability while preserving full lineage back to the original source.
